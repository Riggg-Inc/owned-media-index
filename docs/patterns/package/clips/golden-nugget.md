# Golden Nugget

**Stage:** Package → Clips  
**Score:** 5  
**Evidence:** internal production data

## What It Is

A clip capturing a single, actionable piece of advice or insight that the audience can apply immediately. The value is dense and specific.

## Why It Works

Practitioners share tactics. When someone hears a specific, usable insight, they save it, screenshot it, or send it to a colleague. Golden nuggets earn the most saves and bookmarks.

## Selection Criteria

- One clear, actionable insight
- Advice is specific enough to act on immediately
- No jargon that requires context to understand
- Works for both beginners and experienced practitioners

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the advice requires significant context to understand, or when the insight is too basic for the target audience.
