# Hot Take

**Stage:** Package → Clips  
**Score:** 5  
**Evidence:** internal production data

## What It Is

A clip built around a strong, opinionated statement from the host or guest. The speaker takes a clear position that challenges conventional thinking.

## Why It Works

Opinions create engagement. People share, comment on, and argue with strong takes. A hot take clip earns more impressions than a polished summary because it triggers a reaction.

## Selection Criteria

- Speaker states a clear, bold position
- The take is genuinely contrarian, not just provocative
- Statement can stand alone without 2 minutes of setup
- Energy is high — the speaker believes what they are saying

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the take is uninformed, when it might alienate the target audience, or when the speaker walks it back later in the episode.

## Prompt Template

Copy and customize this prompt to generate this pattern:

```
Identify and extract a Hot Take clip moment from this transcript. Look for a strong, opinionated statement where the speaker takes a clear position that challenges conventional thinking.

Transcript excerpt:
[paste transcript section]

Requirements:
- Statement must be bold and genuinely contrarian
- Can stand alone without context
- Energy is high — speaker believes what they are saying
- 30-90 seconds when spoken
- Provide: start/end timestamps, the quote, and a suggested clip title
```

Replace the bracketed placeholders with your specific details. The more context you provide about your audience, guest, and episode content, the better the output.
