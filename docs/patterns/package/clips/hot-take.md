# Hot Take

**Stage:** Package → Clips  
**Score:** 5  
**Evidence:** internal production data

## What It Is

A clip built around a strong, opinionated statement from the host or guest. The speaker takes a clear position that challenges conventional thinking.

## Why It Works

Opinions create engagement. People share, comment on, and argue with strong takes. A hot take clip earns more impressions than a polished summary because it triggers a reaction.

## Selection Criteria

- Speaker states a clear, bold position
- The take is genuinely contrarian, not just provocative
- Statement can stand alone without 2 minutes of setup
- Energy is high — the speaker believes what they are saying

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the take is uninformed, when it might alienate the target audience, or when the speaker walks it back later in the episode.
