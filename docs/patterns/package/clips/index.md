# Clips

**Stage:** Package

Clips are extracted segments from a full session, typically 30–90 seconds, designed to stand alone as consumable content across social platforms, email, and web.

## Clip Styles

| Style | Best For | Score |
|---|---|---|
| [Hot Take](hot-take.md) | Strong opinions, contrarian moments | 5 |
| [Golden Nugget](golden-nugget.md) | Tactical advice, quotable insights | 5 |
| [Story Beat](story-beat.md) | Guest anecdotes, narrative moments | 4 |
| [Debate Moment](debate-moment.md) | Multi-guest disagreement, tension | 4 |
| [Data Drop](data-drop.md) | Statistics, benchmarks, proof points | 4 |

## Quality Bar

A strong clip should:

- Stand alone without context from the full episode
- Open with energy — no slow ramp-up
- Deliver one clear idea in under 90 seconds
- End with a natural close or curiosity hook
- Work in both horizontal (16:9) and vertical (9:16) formats
