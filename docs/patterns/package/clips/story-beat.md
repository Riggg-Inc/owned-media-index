# Story Beat

**Stage:** Package → Clips  
**Score:** 4  
**Evidence:** internal production data

## What It Is

A clip extracted from a compelling narrative moment — a guest anecdote, origin story, or turning point that creates emotional engagement.

## Why It Works

Stories are memorable. A 60-second story clip lands harder than a 60-second advice clip because it engages emotion, not just intellect.

## Selection Criteria

- Story has a clear arc (setup, tension, resolution)
- Fits within 60-90 seconds without heavy editing
- Emotional beat is genuine, not performed
- Story illustrates a transferable lesson

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the story requires setup from earlier in the conversation, or when the emotional moment does not translate without visual cues.
