# Audiogram Card

**Stage:** Package → Quote Graphics  
**Score:** 3  
**Evidence:** practitioner observation

## What It Is

A static or animated graphic paired with a short audio clip. Combines visual branding with the actual speaker's voice.

## Quality Bar

- Quote must be 30 words or fewer
- Speaker attribution: name, title, company
- Readable at mobile size without zooming
- Brand colors and fonts consistent
- Works at 1:1 and 16:9 aspect ratios

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the audio quality is poor or when the quote works better as text alone.
