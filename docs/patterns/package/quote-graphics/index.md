# Quote Graphics

**Stage:** Package

Quote graphics extract the most shareable moments from a session and package them as static visual assets. They are one of the highest-performing owned media derivatives because they are fast to consume, easy to share, and platform-agnostic.

## Quote Graphic Styles

| Style | Best For | Score |
|---|---|---|
| [Speaker + Quote](speaker-quote.md) | Expert insights, authority moments | 5 |
| [Stat Callout](stat-callout.md) | Data points, benchmarks, proof | 4 |
| [Conversation Exchange](conversation-exchange.md) | Host/guest dialogue, debate moments | 4 |
| [Branded Pull Quote](branded-pull-quote.md) | Series-consistent social assets | 4 |
| [Audiogram Card](audiogram-card.md) | Audio snippets with visual context | 3 |

## Quality Bar

- Quote must stand alone without episode context
- Maximum 2 sentences or 30 words
- Speaker attribution must be clear (name, title, company)
- Brand colors and fonts consistent with show identity
- Works at 1:1 (Instagram/LinkedIn) and 16:9 (Twitter/LinkedIn) sizes
