# Speaker Quote

**Stage:** Package → Quote Graphics  
**Score:** 5  
**Evidence:** practitioner observation

## What It Is

A graphic featuring the speaker's headshot or portrait alongside their quote in bold typography. The most direct and recognizable quote graphic format.

## Quality Bar

- Quote must be 30 words or fewer
- Speaker attribution: name, title, company
- Readable at mobile size without zooming
- Brand colors and fonts consistent
- Works at 1:1 and 16:9 aspect ratios

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the speaker is not recognizable and the quote alone is the stronger asset.

## Prompt Template

Copy and customize this prompt to generate this pattern:

```
Create a quote graphic using the Speaker + Quote pattern.

Context:
- Speaker name: [full name]
- Speaker title: [role, company]
- Quote: [exact quote — max 30 words]
- Speaker headshot: [attach or describe]
- Show brand colors: [hex codes]

Requirements:
- Quote must be 30 words or fewer
- Speaker attribution: name, title, company
- Readable at mobile size
- Both 1:1 and 16:9 versions
- Brand colors and fonts consistent with show identity
```

Replace the bracketed placeholders with your specific details. The more context you provide about your audience, guest, and episode content, the better the output.
