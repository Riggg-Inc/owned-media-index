# Speaker Quote

**Stage:** Package → Quote Graphics  
**Score:** 5  
**Evidence:** practitioner observation

## What It Is

A graphic featuring the speaker's headshot or portrait alongside their quote in bold typography. The most direct and recognizable quote graphic format.

## Quality Bar

- Quote must be 30 words or fewer
- Speaker attribution: name, title, company
- Readable at mobile size without zooming
- Brand colors and fonts consistent
- Works at 1:1 and 16:9 aspect ratios

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the speaker is not recognizable and the quote alone is the stronger asset.
