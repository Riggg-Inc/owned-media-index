# Reels

**Stage:** Package

Reels are vertical (9:16) short-form video assets designed for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and LinkedIn vertical video. Unlike clips, reels are formatted specifically for mobile-first consumption with captions, visual hooks, and platform-native pacing.

## Reel Styles

| Style | Best For | Score |
|---|---|---|
| [Hook-Insight-CTA](hook-insight-cta.md) | Tactical advice, quick wins | 5 |
| [Quote Card Motion](quote-card-motion.md) | Guest sound bites, shareable moments | 4 |
| [Before-After Reveal](before-after-reveal.md) | Transformation stories, visual contrast | 4 |
| [Rapid Fire](rapid-fire.md) | Lists, multiple quick insights | 4 |
| [Teaser Trailer](teaser-trailer.md) | Episode promotion, audience building | 3 |

## Quality Bar

A strong reel should:

- Hook in the first 2 seconds — no logos, no intros
- Use burned-in captions (80%+ of social video is watched without sound)
- Deliver one clear idea in 15–60 seconds
- End with a reason to follow, subscribe, or watch the full episode
- Be natively vertical — not a cropped horizontal video
