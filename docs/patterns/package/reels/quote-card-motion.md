# Quote Card Motion

**Stage:** Package → Reels  
**Score:** 4  
**Evidence:** platform observation

## What It Is

A reel that features a guest quote as animated text over the speaker's video. The quote is the content — the video adds personality and authenticity.

## Why It Works

Quotes are the most shareable unit of owned media. Adding motion and the speaker's face transforms a static quote graphic into a dynamic, platform-native format.

## Quality Bar

- Hook must land in the first 2 seconds
- Captions must be burned in and readable on mobile
- Vertical native (9:16) — never a cropped horizontal video
- One clear idea per reel (except rapid-fire)
- CTA must be specific: follow, subscribe, link in bio, full episode

## When Not To Use

Avoid when the quote is generic or when the speaker's delivery is flat.

## Prompt Template

Copy and customize this prompt to generate this pattern:

```
Write a quote card reel script using the Quote Card Motion pattern.

Context:
- Speaker name: [who said it]
- Speaker title: [their role/company]
- Quote: [the exact quote — max 30 words]
- Episode: [which episode is this from]

Format:
VISUAL: Speaker video with animated quote text overlay
TEXT: [the quote, broken into 2-3 animated lines]
CAPTION: [supporting context + CTA]

Requirements:
- Quote must be 30 words or fewer
- Speaker delivery must have energy
- Include burned-in captions
- 15-30 seconds
```

Replace the bracketed placeholders with your specific details. The more context you provide about your audience, guest, and episode content, the better the output.
