# Thumbnails

**Stage:** Package

Thumbnails are the visual packaging that determines whether someone clicks on your episode in a podcast app, YouTube, or social feed. They are the visual equivalent of the title — the first impression of every asset.

## Thumbnail Styles

| Style | Best For | Score |
|---|---|---|
| [Guest Headshot + Text](guest-headshot-text.md) | Expert interviews, authority episodes | 5 |
| [Emotion Close-Up](emotion-close-up.md) | High-energy episodes, reaction-driven content | 4 |
| [Branded Template](branded-template.md) | Series consistency, show recognition | 4 |
| [Data Visual](data-visual.md) | Benchmark episodes, stat-driven content | 3 |
| [Scene Still](scene-still.md) | Behind-the-scenes, production-focused episodes | 3 |

## Quality Bar

- Readable at 120x120px (podcast app grid size)
- Maximum 5-7 words of text
- Guest face visible when relevant
- Brand colors and show identity consistent
- High contrast between text and background
