## How To Read This

**Top-right (Broad Authority):** Patterns that work for large audiences and lead with credibility. Best for shows with high-profile guests or well-known hosts. *Authority Claim* lives here.

**Top-left (Niche Authority):** Patterns that target specific roles or industries with an authority frame. Best for B2B shows with defined audience segments. *Role Guide* lives here.

**Bottom-right (Broad Curiosity):** Patterns that earn clicks through tension, challenge, or promise. Highest potential reach. Best for growth-stage shows. *Contrarian Hook* and *How-To Outcome* live here.

**Bottom-left (Niche Curiosity):** Patterns that create intellectual curiosity for a specific audience. Best for thought leadership shows. *Big Question* lives here.

## When To Use Which Quadrant

| Your Goal | Pick From |
|---|---|
| Maximize reach / downloads | Bottom-right: Contrarian Hook, How-To Outcome, Numbered Insight |
| Build authority with known guests | Top-right: Authority Claim, Guest Authority |
| Target a specific buyer persona | Top-left: Role Guide |
| Thought leadership positioning | Bottom-left: Big Question, Future Of |
| Versatile / balanced | Center: Inside Look, Before-After, Hidden Cost |
