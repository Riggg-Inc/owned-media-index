# Content Formats

Episode type taxonomy for episodic owned media.

## Core Formats

### Expert Interview

Host-led conversation with a domain expert. Best for authority building and guest-driven distribution.

### Operator Roundtable

Multi-guest discussion around one specific problem or theme. Especially useful for B2B differentiation because it captures peer-level operator insight.

### Solo Explainer

Host teaches one concept end to end. Lower production overhead and strong for point-of-view content.

### Case Study Breakdown

Deconstructs a client, company, or industry example. Strong trust signal when handled with specificity and care.

### Tactical Briefing

Short episode under 15 minutes focused on one action the listener can execute immediately. Strong fit for clips, email, and internal enablement.
