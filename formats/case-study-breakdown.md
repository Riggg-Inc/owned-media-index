# Case Study Breakdown

Deconstructed client or industry example.

## What This Is For

Case study breakdowns turn real examples into trust-building educational content.

## When To Use It

Use this format when:

- A real example can teach the lesson better than theory.
- The company needs credibility.
- The audience cares about practical application.
- Sensitive information can be handled responsibly.

## What Good Looks Like

Strong case study breakdowns include:

- Anonymization guidelines where needed.
- Problem-to-approach-to-result structure.
- Clear constraints and tradeoffs.
- Data visualization standards where relevant.
