# Expert Interview

Primary format for authority building.

## What This Is For

A host interviews a domain expert to surface useful insight, perspective, and credibility.

## When To Use It

Use this format when:

- Guest expertise is the draw.
- Relationship building matters.
- The audience wants credible outside perspective.
- Guest-share distribution is part of the strategy.

## What Good Looks Like

Strong expert interviews include:

- Pre-interview research doc.
- Structured question bank.
- Guest onboarding checklist.
- Clear discussion arc.
- Post-interview recap for repurposing.
