# Packaging Standards

Standards for titles, summaries, timestamps, and episode packaging.

## What This Is For

Packaging helps the audience understand why an episode matters before they listen or watch.

## When To Use It

Use this for every episode before publishing.

## What Good Looks Like

Strong packaging includes:

- Clear title.
- Search-aware summary.
- Key takeaways.
- Timestamps and chapter markers.
- Guest bio.
- Resource links.
- Clear CTA.
