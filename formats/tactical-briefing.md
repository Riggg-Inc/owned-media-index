# Tactical Briefing

Short episode focused on one action.

## What This Is For

Tactical briefings help the listener do one specific thing immediately.

## When To Use It

Use this format when:

- The idea is narrow.
- Speed and usefulness matter.
- The content should work well in email or social.
- The topic does not need a long conversation.

## What Good Looks Like

Strong tactical briefings include:

- Under 15 minute runtime.
- Single-thesis outline.
- One clear action.
- Clip-first editing approach.
