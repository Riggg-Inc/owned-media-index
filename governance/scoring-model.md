# Scoring Model

The index scores tools and workflows from 1 to 5.

## Scores

### 5 - Industry Standard

Recommended default. Strong fit for most serious teams.

### 4 - Strong Fit

Used regularly in Riggg workflows or comparable production environments. Strong choice with known tradeoffs.

### 3 - Viable Option

Works, but with meaningful tradeoffs. Suitable for some teams.

### 2 - Niche Case

Useful for a specific use case, budget, workflow, or constraint.

### 1 - Not Recommended

Included for completeness or context. Not recommended as a default.

## Scoring Criteria

Consider:

- Reliability.
- Quality.
- Ease of use.
- Collaboration.
- Export/portability.
- Value.
- Support.
- Fit for real production workflows.
