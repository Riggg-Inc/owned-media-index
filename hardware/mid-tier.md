# Mid-Tier

Prosumer setup from $500-$2,000.

## What This Is For

The mid-tier is for recurring hosts and teams who produce regularly and need better quality, control, and consistency.

## When To Use It

Use this tier when:

- The show is recurring.
- The host appears on camera frequently.
- Audio quality is part of the brand.
- The team wants reliable repeatable production.

## What Good Looks Like

A good mid-tier setup should include:

- Dynamic XLR microphone.
- Audio interface or recorder.
- Closed-back monitoring headphones.
- Dedicated camera or high-quality webcam.
- Key light and fill/control.
- Basic acoustic treatment.
