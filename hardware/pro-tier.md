# Pro Tier

Studio-grade setup above $2,000.

## What This Is For

The pro tier is for studios, executive media teams, high-value shows, and teams where production quality is a strategic brand signal.

## When To Use It

Use this tier when:

- The show represents the company publicly.
- Multiple people record in one room.
- Live production or switching is required.
- Lighting, camera quality, and sound consistency matter.

## What Good Looks Like

A good pro setup may include:

- Broadcast-quality microphones.
- Multi-channel audio interface or mixer.
- Cinema or studio cameras.
- Controlled lighting.
- Video switcher/capture system.
- Treated room.
- Redundant recording path.
