# Starter Tier

Budget setup under $500.

## What This Is For

The starter tier is for hosts, guests, and small teams who need a meaningful quality upgrade without building a studio.

## When To Use It

Use this tier when:

- Budget is limited.
- Recording is remote.
- Simplicity matters more than maximum control.
- The priority is clear speech and stable video.

## What Good Looks Like

A good starter setup should include:

- USB microphone or simple dynamic mic/interface combo.
- Closed-back headphones.
- Reliable webcam or phone camera.
- Basic key light.
- Simple room treatment or soft furnishings.
