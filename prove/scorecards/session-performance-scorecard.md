# Session Performance Scorecard

## What It Is

A scorecard for evaluating how a single session performed across production, packaging, publishing, and business outcomes.

## Best For

- Podcasts.
- Webinars.
- Livestreams.
- Virtual event sessions.
- Recurring owned media programs.

## Why It Works

Owned media performance is not one metric. A session can succeed through reach, engagement, conversion, reuse, sales enablement, or long-term search value.

## Suggested Categories

- Production quality.
- Packaging completeness.
- Publishing completion.
- Reach.
- Engagement.
- Clicks.
- Conversion events.
- Sales/customer usefulness.
- Reuse potential.
- Library value.

## Quality Bar

The scorecard should help the team decide what to repeat, improve, or stop doing.

## When Not To Use

Avoid over-scoring early programs with little data. Use lightweight qualitative review until enough baseline exists.
