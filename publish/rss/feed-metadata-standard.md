# RSS Feed Metadata Standard

## What It Is

A standard for podcast RSS metadata that supports discoverability, clarity, and distribution quality.

## Best For

- Podcast feeds.
- Audio-first shows.
- Video podcasts distributed through RSS.

## Why It Works

RSS metadata travels across listening platforms. Weak metadata makes the episode harder to understand, find, and share.

## Required Fields

- Episode title.
- Episode description.
- Publish date.
- Audio file.
- Duration.
- Explicit status.
- Episode artwork where applicable.
- Episode number or season where applicable.
- Guest name where applicable.
- Canonical website link.

## Quality Bar

The metadata should make the episode understandable inside a podcast app without requiring the listener to visit another page.

## When Not To Use

Do not treat RSS as the only home for the episode. The canonical owned website page should still exist.
