# Release Checklist

Per-episode QA and go-live checklist.

## Before Release

- Confirm episode title.
- Confirm description and summary.
- Confirm guest name, bio, and links.
- Confirm transcript and timestamps.
- Confirm audio/video playback.
- Confirm thumbnail or cover image.
- Confirm CTA.
- Confirm canonical page URL.
- Confirm RSS metadata.
- Confirm YouTube chapters.
- Confirm social copy and clips.

## After Release

- Verify live page.
- Verify podcast platform ingestion.
- Verify YouTube upload.
- Verify social posts.
- Verify email send.
- Verify analytics events.
- Save guest-share package.
