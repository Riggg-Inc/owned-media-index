# Repurposing Pipeline

Recommended derivative asset package from one episode.

## Minimum Asset Set

From one episode, generate:

- 3 short-form video clips for YouTube Shorts, Reels, and TikTok.
- 2 quote cards with guest headshot where appropriate.
- 1 written summary, 500 to 800 words, for blog or episode page.
- 1 episode email.
- 5 to 10 social copy variants.
- Guest-share package with link, clip, quote card, and bio.

## Quality Bar

Derivative assets should stand alone while still pointing back to the canonical episode page.
