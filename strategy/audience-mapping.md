# Audience Mapping

Define and validate the audience for an episodic owned media asset.

## What This Is For

Audience mapping helps teams avoid creating content for a vague or imaginary listener.

## When To Use It

Use this during show strategy, format selection, guest selection, and content planning.

## What Good Looks Like

A useful audience map includes:

- Primary audience.
- Secondary audience.
- Audience pain points.
- Desired transformation.
- Buying role or influence.
- Existing content habits.
- Questions they repeatedly ask.
