# Business Outcomes

Connect episodic owned media to measurable business goals.

## What This Is For

Podcasts and owned media programs should serve a business purpose beyond "content."

## When To Use It

Use this before launching a show and during quarterly strategy reviews.

## What Good Looks Like

Common outcomes include:

- Lead generation.
- SEO growth.
- Authority positioning.
- Sales enablement.
- Customer education.
- Recruiting.
- Community building.
- Partner development.
