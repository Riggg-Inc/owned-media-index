# Show Concept

Define the strategic foundation for an episodic owned media asset.

## What This Is For

Use this document to clarify the audience, positioning, format, promise, and business role of a podcast or recurring media program before production begins.

## When To Use It

Use this before choosing tools, hardware, guests, or publishing cadence.

## What Good Looks Like

A strong show concept defines:

- Target audience.
- Problem or opportunity the show addresses.
- Point of view.
- Host role.
- Format.
- Cadence.
- Business outcome.
- Primary call to action.
